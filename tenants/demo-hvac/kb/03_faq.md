# Frequently Asked Questions

**My AC isn't working at all — how fast can someone come?**
No AC is treated as an emergency. We'll try to get someone out the same day, and
our dispatch team is notified right away so they can prioritize you.

**Is the diagnostic fee separate from the repair cost?**
The $99 diagnostic fee is waived if you book the repair with us. If you decide not
to do the repair, the $99 covers the tech's visit and assessment.

**How much is a refrigerant recharge?**
Typically $150-$300 depending on refrigerant type and how much your system needs.
The tech confirms the exact cost on-site before doing any work.

**My unit is old — should I repair it or replace it?**
It depends on the age and condition. As a rule of thumb, units 10+ years old are
often worth a replacement conversation — our team can go over the options with you.

**Do you have a 24/7 emergency line?**
Yes — emergency calls are accepted any time, day or night.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Cash, all major credit/debit cards, and financing for larger jobs like full system
replacements.

**Where do you service?**
South Florida, residential properties only.

**What are your hours for scheduled visits?**
Monday – Saturday, 7 AM to 7 PM. Emergency line is 24/7.
