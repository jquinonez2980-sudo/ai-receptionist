# Frequently Asked Questions - Orchelix AI Consulting

## About the AI Receptionist Agent (Our Flagship Specialty)

**Q: What exactly is the Orchelix AI Receptionist Agent?**  
A: It is a production-ready, multi-channel AI agent that acts as your business’s always-on receptionist. It handles all inbound inquiries via email, SMS, website chat, and WhatsApp, qualifies leads, answers FAQs, books appointments, follows up on no-shows, and escalates complex cases to a human.

**Q: Which channels does the AI Receptionist support?**  
A: Email, SMS, live website chat, and WhatsApp. It can be added to any of these channels quickly and works seamlessly across them.

**Q: How does the agent qualify leads?**  
A: It asks smart, natural conversational questions based on your business rules and knowledge base to determine lead quality, interest level, and readiness to book. Only qualified leads are booked or escalated.

**Q: Can it book appointments directly?**  
A: Yes. It checks your real-time calendar availability and books appointments instantly into Google Calendar, Calendly, or your preferred system.

**Q: What happens if a lead has a complex or high-value question?**  
A: The agent intelligently escalates the conversation to a human team member with full context already provided, so nothing falls through the cracks.

**Q: Does the agent remember previous conversations?**  
A: Yes. It is fully stateful and built with LangGraph’s graph-based orchestration, so it maintains context across messages, channels, and days.

**Q: How fast can you deploy the AI Receptionist Agent?**  
A: Most clients are fully live within 5–10 business days after the free audit.
