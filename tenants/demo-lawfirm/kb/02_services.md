# Services & Pricing

## Consultation — $250/hour
Direct time with the attorney to discuss your immigration or family law matter
and understand your options. This is where specific legal questions get
answered — not before.

## Case Review — $500 flat fee
A thorough review of existing documents, filings, or a prior case, with the
attorney's assessment of next steps.

## Payment Plans
Payment plans are available for clients who need them. Ask the office for
details when you book.

## What we don't do over chat or phone
We never give legal advice, estimate odds of winning a case, or interpret
documents outside of a paid consultation or case review with the attorney.
