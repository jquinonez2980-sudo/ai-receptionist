# Services & Pricing

## New Patient Exam & X-Rays — $150
Comprehensive exam with the dentist plus a full set of digital X-rays. Includes a
personalized treatment plan. Recommended for anyone new to our office.
Duration: ~60 minutes.

## Routine Cleaning — $120
Professional cleaning, polish, and gum health check for existing patients on a
regular schedule.
Duration: ~45-60 minutes.

## Teeth Whitening — $350
In-office professional whitening treatment. Results are visible after a single visit.

## Crown — $1,200
Custom-fitted permanent crown to restore a damaged, cracked, or heavily decayed
tooth. The exact cost is confirmed after the dentist examines the tooth — this is
the starting price for a standard crown.

## Invisalign Consultation — Free
No-cost, no-obligation consultation to see if Invisalign is right for you. Includes
a personalized treatment estimate.

## Other treatment
Root canals, extractions, implants, and multi-tooth treatment plans are priced
individually after an exam — we never quote those over the phone or chat.

## Insurance & Payment
We accept most major dental insurance plans. Our team verifies your benefits before
your visit. Payment plans are available for larger treatment. Cash and all major
cards accepted.
