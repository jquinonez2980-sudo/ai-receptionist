# Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I just book a Botox appointment directly?**
Botox and filler always start with a consultation with one of our providers —
that's how we make sure the treatment and dosage are right for you. We get that
on the calendar first.

**How much is Botox?**
It's billed at $12 per unit, with most treatments running $300-$600 depending on
the area. Your provider will confirm the exact amount during your consultation.

**Will this make me look younger / get rid of all my wrinkles?**
Results vary from person to person. Your provider will talk through what to
realistically expect for you during your consultation.

**Is laser hair removal permanent?**
It provides long-term hair reduction; results vary by person and skin/hair type.
A series of sessions is typically recommended.

**I'm pregnant / on medication — can I still get treatment?**
That's an important question for your provider — we'll make sure it's covered in
your consultation. We can also have a provider reach out to you directly if you'd
like to discuss it before booking.

**Do you speak Spanish?**
Yes — hablamos español. Our team is fully bilingual.

**What are your hours?**
Tuesday – Saturday, 10 AM to 6 PM. Closed Sunday and Monday.

**Can I cancel or reschedule?**
Yes — just reach out. We appreciate at least 24 hours notice so we can offer the
slot to another client.
