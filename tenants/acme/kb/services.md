# Acme Dental Care — Services

Acme Dental Care is a family and cosmetic dental practice in Brooklyn, New York.
We have served the neighborhood for over 20 years.

## General Dentistry
- Cleanings and exams
- Fillings, crowns, and bridges
- Root canals
- Gum disease treatment

## Cosmetic Dentistry
- Teeth whitening
- Veneers
- Invisalign clear aligners

## Hours
Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Closed weekends.

## Insurance
We accept most major PPO dental insurance plans and offer in-house financing
for treatments not covered by insurance.

## New Patients
New patients are always welcome. Your first visit includes a comprehensive exam,
digital X-rays, and a professional cleaning.
