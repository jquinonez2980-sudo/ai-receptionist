# Frequently Asked Questions

**Can you tell me if I'll win my case?**
No one can promise an outcome, and we never estimate odds over chat or phone.
The attorney will go over your specific situation during a consultation.

**I have a court date this week / I'm dealing with detention — what do I do?**
This is treated as urgent. Let us know right away and we'll make sure the
attorney is notified immediately, while still working to get you the soonest
consultation slot.

**How much does a consultation cost?**
$250 per hour, billed for time with the attorney. Case reviews are a $500 flat
fee. Payment plans are available.

**Do I need an appointment?**
Yes — all attorney time is by appointment (consultation or case review).

**Do you speak Spanish?**
Yes — hablamos español (español latinoamericano). 

**What are your hours?**
Monday – Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM. Closed Saturday and Sunday.

**Can I cancel or reschedule?**
Yes — just reach out. We appreciate as much notice as possible so we can offer
the time to another client.

**What should I have ready before my consultation?**
Any relevant documents (notices, prior filings, court paperwork) are helpful,
but don't delay booking if you don't have everything — bring what you have.
