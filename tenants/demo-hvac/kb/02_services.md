# Services & Pricing

## AC Tune-Up — $89
Full system inspection and cleaning. Recommended once a year to catch small
issues before they become expensive ones.

## Diagnostic Visit — $99
A certified tech diagnoses what's wrong with your system. The $99 fee is waived
if you book the repair with us.

## Refrigerant Recharge — $150-$300
Price depends on refrigerant type (R-22 vs R-410A) and how much your system
needs. Exact cost is confirmed on-site before any work begins.

## Emergency Call — $199 + parts
For no AC, no heat, or any urgent system failure. $199 covers the emergency
service call; parts and any repair work are additional and quoted on-site.
Available 24/7.

## Replacement systems
We also install full system replacements. Pricing depends on the home and
equipment — our team will go over options on-site, especially for units 10+
years old where replacement may be more cost-effective than continued repairs.

## Payment
Cash, all major cards, and financing options available for larger jobs.
