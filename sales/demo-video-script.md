# Esmi Demo Video Scripts

Two cuts. Record with Loom (screen + webcam bubble) or a phone. The 2-minute is for the
landing page + cold emails; the 30-second is for IG/FB/ads and DMs.

**Recording setup (do this once):**
- Spin up a demo tenant for a realistic local business (e.g. `tenants/sunshine-hvac/`) so the
  on-screen chat is branded and concrete — see `demo-tenant-checklist.md`.
- Have two windows ready: the chat at `orchelix.com/try-esmi?tenant=sunshine-hvac`, and a Google
  Calendar (empty) so the booked appointment visibly appears.
- For the voice beat, call **561-566-1066** on speaker and screen-record the call audio, or just
  narrate over the web chat.
- Keep it raw and real. A slightly imperfect live demo out-converts a polished fake one.

## 🎬 The 2-minute demo (landing page + email)

> Total ~2:00. Word count ~300. Speak conversationally, like you're showing a friend.

**[0:00–0:10 — HOOK]**
*On screen:* a phone ringing, then "Call ended — voicemail."
> "This is a local business losing about eight thousand dollars a month — and they have no idea.
> Here's why."

**[0:10–0:30 — THE PROBLEM]**
*On screen:* clock showing 7:42 PM; a "Missed call" notification; Google search results for
"plumber near me" with competitors.
> "One in four calls to a home-services business goes unanswered — after hours, during a job, at
> lunch. And when you don't pick up, that customer doesn't wait. They call the next guy on Google.
> Every missed call is a job you already lost."

**[0:30–1:10 — THE MAGIC MOMENT]**
*On screen:* the branded chat console. Type: *"My AC stopped working, can someone come out?"*
Let Esmi respond live — ask the day, show calendar slots, book it.
> "So we built Esmi. She answers every single call, 24/7, the instant it comes in. Watch — a
> customer says their AC is down…"
*(Esmi checks the calendar, offers slots, books the appointment.)*
> "…she reads the live calendar, books the appointment, and confirms it by text. No staff, no
> hold music, no voicemail. And here's the part that matters in South Florida —"
*On screen:* type in Spanish: *"¿Cuánto cobran por una visita?"* — Esmi answers in Spanish.
> "— she does all of it in English and Spanish, automatically."
*On screen:* cut to the Google Calendar — the new appointment appears.
> "That appointment? It's already on the calendar."

**[1:10–1:35 — THE ROI]**
*On screen:* simple text — "Human receptionist: ~$3,000/mo · 40 hrs · English only" vs
"Esmi: 24/7 · EN + ES · books the job."
> "A human receptionist costs three to four thousand a month, works forty hours, and goes home at
> five. Esmi works every hour of every day, never takes a sick day, and costs a fraction of that.
> One recovered job usually pays for the whole month."

**[1:35–2:00 — THE CTA]**
*On screen:* your face (webcam). Big text: `orchelix.com/try-esmi` and `561-566-1066`.
> "Here's what I'll do: I'll build a version of Esmi trained on *your* business — your services,
> your pricing — for free. Then you call it yourself and try to break it. Go to orchelix dot com
> slash try-esmi and talk to her right now, or call the number on your screen. If you're tired of
> losing calls, let's fix it this week."

## 🎬 The 30-second cut (IG / FB / ads / DMs)

> Fast, punchy, captioned (most watch muted — burn in captions).

**[0:00–0:05]** *On screen:* "7:42 PM. Customer calls. You're on a job." → voicemail icon.
> "After-hours calls go to voicemail. That customer just called your competitor."

**[0:05–0:18]** *On screen:* branded chat — "My AC is out, can someone come today?" → Esmi books it.
> "Esmi answers every call 24/7, in English and Spanish, and books the job for you — automatically."

**[0:18–0:25]** *On screen:* calendar appointment appears + SMS confirmation.
> "Booked. Confirmed by text. While you're still on the roof."

**[0:25–0:30]** *On screen:* `orchelix.com/try-esmi`.
> "Try her free, trained on your business. Link in bio."
