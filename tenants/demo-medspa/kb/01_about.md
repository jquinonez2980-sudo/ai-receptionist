# About Glow Medical Spa

Glow Medical Spa is a medical spa and aesthetics practice in South Florida,
offering injectables, skin treatments, laser hair removal, and body contouring
in a relaxed, welcoming setting. All injectable treatments (Botox, filler) start
with a consultation with one of our licensed providers.

We are bilingual — English and Spanish spoken.
