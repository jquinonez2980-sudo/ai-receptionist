# FAQ — Coastline Condos

## Pricing
**Q: What do units cost?**  
A: Available 2-bedroom residences start from **$90,000** (units 101, 102, 201, 202).
Unit **103** (3BR) starts from **$120,000**. Units **301** and **302** are sold;
**203** is conditionally sold. Call `get_pricing` for the structured list.
Full packages and payment schedules come through VIP registration or a sales consult.

## Payment plans
**Q: Do you offer financing / payment plans?**  
A: Flexible **pre-construction payment schedules** are available, including staged
payments during construction. Exact schedules are tailored by the sales team after
VIP registration or a tour.

## Availability
**Q: Which units are still available?**  
A: Available: **101, 102, 103, 201, 202**. Conditionally sold: **203**. Sold: **301, 302**.

## Location
**Q: Where is the project?**  
A: Km 5 Vía Data, Playas (General Villamil), Ecuador — one block from the beach,
about 1.5–2 hours from Guayaquil airport.

## Foreign buyers
**Q: Can foreigners buy?**  
A: Yes — international buyers are welcome. The team supports remote buyers with
video tours and guided next steps. For legal closing details, escalate to human sales.

## Tours
**Q: Can I visit?**  
A: Yes. Book a private on-site visit or request a video tour. Collect preferred
dates, name, and WhatsApp/email, then book a consultation slot or escalate to sales.
