# About Coastline Condos

Coastline Condos is a boutique 3-story ocean-view condominium development in
Playas (General Villamil), Ecuador. Tagline: **Oceanfront Living, Elevated.**

The project sits just **one block from the Pacific beach** at **Km 5 Vía Data**.
Residences feature private balconies/terraces, modern coastal design, and
panoramic ocean views.
