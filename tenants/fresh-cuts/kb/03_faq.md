# Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need an appointment?**
Walk-ins are always welcome. But appointment clients are always seen first, so if you want
to skip the wait, book ahead.

**How long is the wait for walk-ins?**
It depends on the day. Saturdays can have 30-60 minute waits. Weekday mornings are usually quick.
Book an appointment to guarantee your time.

**Can I request a specific barber?**
Appointments are with the shop, not a specific barber. We'll make sure you get a great cut.

**Do you take kids?**
Yes — kids 12 and under are $25. We're patient and kid-friendly.

**Do you do beard trims without a haircut?**
Yes — beard lineup only is $15.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Cash and all major credit/debit cards. No checks.

**Do you speak Spanish?**
Yes — hablamos español. Our team is fully bilingual.

**Where are you located?**
Boca Raton, FL. Ask when booking for the exact address.

**What are your hours?**
Monday – Saturday, 9 AM to 7 PM. Closed Sunday.

**Can I cancel or reschedule?**
Yes — just call or text us. We appreciate at least 2 hours notice so we can offer the slot
to another client.
