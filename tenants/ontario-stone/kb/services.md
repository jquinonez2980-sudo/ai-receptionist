## Services

### Kitchen Countertops
We fabricate and install kitchen countertops in granite, quartz, and marble. Every job includes
a free in-home measurement and quote. Average turnaround from signed contract to installation
is 7–10 business days. We handle the full job — templating, fabrication, and installation.

### Bathroom Vanity Tops
Single and double vanity tops available in all materials. Custom edge profiles and cutouts for
undermount sinks. Turnaround is typically 5–7 business days from signed contract.

### Outdoor Kitchens & BBQ Countertops
We specialize in outdoor kitchen surfaces designed for Ontario's climate. Granite and quartzite
are recommended for outdoor use — freeze-thaw resistant and heat tolerant. Seasonal installation
available spring through fall.

### Commercial Projects
We work with contractors, developers, and designers on commercial countertop projects — offices,
restaurants, condo buildings, hotel renovations. Please book a consultation for commercial pricing.

## Materials
- **Granite** — natural stone, unique patterns, very durable. Most popular for kitchens.
- **Quartz** (engineered) — consistent colour and pattern, non-porous, low maintenance.
  Popular for bathrooms and lighter kitchen designs.
- **Marble** — elegant and classic, best for lower-traffic areas like bathroom vanities.
- **Quartzite** — natural stone, harder than marble, excellent for outdoor applications.

We carry slabs in stock at our fabrication shop and can source special orders from our
distributors across Ontario.

## Pricing
Pricing depends on material, square footage, edge profile, and number of cutouts. We do not
quote over the phone without a measurement — every slab and every kitchen layout is different.
We offer **free in-home measurements and written quotes** with no obligation.

General range: kitchen countertops typically run $60–$150 per square foot installed, depending
on material and complexity. Quartz and granite are at the lower end; exotic marbles and
quartzites at the higher end. All pricing is in Canadian dollars.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does installation take?**
The installation itself takes 2–4 hours for a typical kitchen. We're in and out in a day.

**Do you remove the old countertops?**
Yes — old countertop removal and disposal is included in the installation price.

**Do you need a plumber for the sink?**
We disconnect and reconnect standard sink plumbing in most cases. Complex plumbing may
require a separate plumber — we'll flag this during the quote.

**Can I see slabs before choosing?**
Yes. Once an appointment is booked, we can arrange a visit to our fabrication shop to
view slabs in person before making a final selection.

**Do you work with designers and contractors?**
Yes — we regularly partner with residential designers, architects, and general contractors
across the GTA. Trade pricing is available. Contact us to discuss a trade account.

**Do you speak languages other than English?**
Yes. Our receptionist Esmi speaks English, Farsi, Mandarin, Spanish, and Italian. Just write
or speak to her in your preferred language and she will respond the same way. This applies to
booking appointments, asking about pricing, and any other questions.
