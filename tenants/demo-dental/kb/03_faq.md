# Frequently Asked Questions

**Do you accept my insurance?**
We accept most major dental insurance plans. Give us your provider and member ID
and our team will verify your benefits before your visit.

**What if I don't have insurance?**
That's no problem — we still see you. Ask about our payment plans for larger
treatment.

**I'm in pain — can I be seen today?**
Yes. Dental pain, swelling, or a broken tooth is treated as an emergency — we will
try to get you a same-day appointment and notify the office right away.

**How much will my treatment cost?**
The five core services (exam, cleaning, whitening, crown, Invisalign consult) have
fixed prices. Anything more involved (root canals, extractions, implants) is quoted
after the dentist examines you — we never guess over the phone.

**Do you see kids?**
Yes, we see patients of all ages.

**Do you speak Spanish?**
Yes — hablamos español. Our team is fully bilingual.

**Where are you located?**
Boca Raton, FL. Ask when booking for the exact address.

**What are your hours?**
Monday – Saturday, 8 AM to 5 PM. Closed Sunday.

**Can I cancel or reschedule?**
Yes — just call or message us. We appreciate at least 24 hours notice so we can
offer the slot to another patient.
