# FAQ

**Q: Do I need an appointment?**
A: Walk-ins are always welcome. To guarantee a time, book Monday–Friday or Sunday. Saturdays are walk-in only.

**Q: How long is the wait on weekends?**
A: Weekends are busiest, especially Saturdays. Come early, or visit during the week for the shortest wait.

**Q: Is parking free?**
A: Yes — free parking at both Weston and Keele.

**Q: Do you cut kids' and babies' hair?**
A: Yes at both locations. Our barbers are patient with little ones.

**Q: Do I need a deposit?**
A: No deposit and no cancellation fee. A courtesy heads-up is appreciated if plans change.

**Q: Are you open on holidays?**
A: Open most statutory holidays. Closed Christmas Day and New Year's Day.

**Q: ¿Hablan español?**
A: ¡Claro que sí! Barbers and Esmi speak Spanish and English.

**Q: Which location should I go to?**
A: Whichever is more convenient — Weston Road (Toronto) or Keele Street (North York). Hours and some prices differ slightly.

**Q: How much is a fade?**
A: Weston $50 · Keele $35–$40. Both about 45 minutes.

**Q: What's the VIP Package?**
A: Weston only — $70, about 1 hour 15 minutes. Premium full treatment.

**Q: Beard trims?**
A: $20 at both locations.
