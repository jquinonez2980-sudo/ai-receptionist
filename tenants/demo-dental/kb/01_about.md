# About Boca Family Dental

Boca Family Dental is a general dentistry practice in Boca Raton, FL, serving
patients of all ages — from kids' first checkups to seniors' restorative care.
We focus on preventive care, comfortable cleanings, and clear, honest treatment
plans with no surprise costs.

We accept most major dental insurance plans and offer payment plans for larger
treatment.
