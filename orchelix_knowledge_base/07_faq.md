She picks up in under 14 seconds, holds a real conversation in English or Spanish, and books the appointment, escalates the urgency, or files the voicemail — with a transcript and a reason for every call, 24/7 including nights, weekends, and holidays.

**Q: What can Esmi do?**
A: Esmi can:
- Answer calls in under 14 seconds, 24/7
- Converse natively in English and Spanish (mid-call language switching); French is optional
- Book directly into Google Calendar, Microsoft 365, Calendly, or Acuity with SMS confirmation
- Route after-hours emergencies to the on-call person within 60 seconds
- Deliver a transcript and structured summary for every call to your inbox or CRM
- Be coached in plain English — update her script by editing a document, no engineer needed

**Q: What results has Esmi delivered?**
A: Pilot outcomes: 412 calls handled in first month, 14-second median pickup including nights and weekends, 87% resolved without ever paging a human.

**Q: Does Esmi remember context across a conversation?**
A: Yes. Esmi is fully stateful — she maintains context within a conversation and across sessions.
