# Our Services — Orchelix AI Consulting

## The Three Agents

### Agent 01 — Esmi · Virtual Receptionist
**"Answers, qualifies, and books — bilingually, 24/7."**

Esmi is the front desk that never closes. She picks up in under fifteen seconds, holds a real conversation in English or Spanish, and books the appointment, escalates the urgency, or files the voicemail — with a transcript and a reason for every call.

**What Esmi does:**
- 14-second pickup, 24/7 — nights, weekends, holidays, before the third ring
- Native EN · ES with mid-call language switching; French optional
- Books directly into Google Calendar, Microsoft 365, Calendly, or Acuity — confirmed with SMS reminder
- Urgency routing — after-hours emergencies reach the on-call person within 60 seconds
- Transcript and structured summary for every call, delivered to your inbox or CRM
- Coachable in plain English — update her script by editing a doc, no engineer needed

**Esmi pilot outcomes:**
- 412 calls handled in first month
- 14-second median pickup including nights and weekends
- 87% resolved without ever paging a human

**Finance OS outcomes:**
- 3.4× faster month-end close after one quarter
