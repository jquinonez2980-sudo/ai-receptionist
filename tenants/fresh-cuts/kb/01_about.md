# About Fresh Cuts Barbershop

Fresh Cuts Barbershop is a classic men's barbershop in Boca Raton, FL. We specialize in
fades, tapers, scissor cuts, beard trims, and straight razor shaves. We serve men, teens,
and kids in a relaxed, no-rush environment.

Walk-ins are always welcome, but appointment clients are always seen first.

We are bilingual — English and Spanish spoken.
