# South Florida Stone Works — Services & FAQ

South Florida Stone Works is a stone fabrication and installation company serving Miami-Dade,
Broward, and Palm Beach counties. We fabricate and install custom granite, quartz, and marble
countertops for residential and commercial projects.

We are bilingual — English and Spanish. Hablamos español.

## Services

### Kitchen Countertops
We fabricate and install kitchen countertops in granite, quartz, and marble. Every job includes
a free in-home measurement and quote. Average turnaround from signed contract to installation
is 7–10 business days. We handle the full job — templating, fabrication, and installation.

### Bathroom Vanity Tops
Single and double vanity tops available in all materials. Custom edge profiles and cutouts for
undermount sinks. Turnaround is 5–7 business days from signed contract.

### Outdoor Kitchens & BBQ Countertops
We specialize in outdoor kitchen surfaces for South Florida's climate. Granite and quartzite
are recommended for outdoor use — UV resistant and heat tolerant. We've done hundreds of
outdoor kitchens across the tri-county area.

### Commercial Projects
We work with contractors and developers on commercial countertop projects — offices, restaurants,
hotel renovations. Please call or book a consultation for commercial pricing.

## Materials
- **Granite** — natural stone, unique patterns, very durable. Most popular for kitchens.
- **Quartz** (engineered) — consistent color and pattern, non-porous, low maintenance. Popular
  for bathrooms and light-colored kitchens.
- **Marble** — elegant and classic, best for lower-traffic areas like bathroom vanities.
- **Quartzite** — natural stone, harder than marble, great for outdoor use.

We carry slabs in stock and can also source special orders from our distributors in Miami.

## Pricing
Pricing depends on material, square footage, edge profile, and number of cutouts. We do not
quote over the phone without a measurement — every slab and every kitchen is different. We
offer **free in-home measurements and written quotes** with no obligation. Book a measurement
appointment and we'll give you an exact number.

General range: kitchen countertops typically run $45–$120 per square foot installed, depending
on material and complexity. Quartz and granite are at the lower end; exotic marbles at the higher end.

## Service Area
Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties. We do not currently serve areas outside South Florida.

## How to Get Started
1. Book a free in-home measurement — Esmi can schedule this right now.
2. We come out, measure, and send a written quote within 24 hours.
3. Once you approve the quote and choose your slab, we order and fabricate.
4. We install, seal, and clean up. The countertop is ready to use immediately.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does installation take?**
The installation itself takes 2–4 hours for a typical kitchen. We're in and out in a day.

**Do you remove the old countertops?**
Yes, old countertop removal and disposal is included in the installation.

**Do you need a plumber?**
We disconnect and reconnect the sink plumbing in most cases. Complex plumbing situations may
require a separate plumber — we'll let you know during the quote.

**Can I come see slabs before choosing?**
Yes. Once you have an appointment booked, we can arrange a visit to our fabrication shop in
[Miami] to view slabs in person before making a final selection.

**Do you offer financing?**
We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards. We do not currently offer in-house financing,
but we can recommend third-party options.

**What if I have a chip or crack after installation?**
We stand behind our work. Call us — minor chips on natural stone can usually be repaired.
Damage from misuse is evaluated case by case.
