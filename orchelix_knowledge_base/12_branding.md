# Brand Guidelines - Orchelix AI Consulting

## Brand Overview
**Orchelix AI Consulting** represents intelligent, reliable, and human-centered AI automation. Our brand is premium, professional, calm, and trustworthy — never hype-heavy or overly technical.

We specialize in the **AI Receptionist Agent**, so all branding reinforces the idea of a sophisticated, always-on receptionist that feels like a seamless extension of the business.

## Logo
- **Primary Logo**: Ultra-simple double-helix symbol (two elegant curving lines) in navy blue with one subtle teal accent.
- **Standalone Icon**: The clean helix can be used alone (favicon, profile pictures, etc.).
- **Full Wordmark**: “ORCHELIX AI CONSULTING” in bold, modern sans-serif uppercase.
- **Usage**: Always use high-contrast on white or light backgrounds. Keep plenty of white space.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Navy Blue** (#0A2540) — Trust, professionalism, authority
- **Accent Teal** (#00B8D4) — Intelligence, calm innovation, approachability
- **Secondary Neutral** — White / light gray backgrounds for clean, premium feel

## Tone of Voice
- **Professional yet approachable** — Confident, clear, and helpful
- **Benefit-first** — Always lead with outcomes (more appointments, less manual work, more revenue)
- **Calm and reassuring** — Never salesy or pushy. Use language that makes the prospect feel understood and in control.
- **Concise and precise** — Short sentences. Avoid jargon unless explaining LangGraph or technical details simply.
- **Human-centered** — Speak like a trusted advisor, not a robot.

### Voice Examples
- Good: “Our AI Receptionist Agent can qualify your leads and book appointments 24/7, so you never miss another opportunity.”
- Avoid: “Revolutionary AI that will 10x your leads overnight!!!”

## Key Messaging Pillars
1. **Reliable Automation** — “Production-ready, stateful, LangGraph-powered”
2. **Fast ROI** — “Visible results in days, not months”
3. **Lead-to-Appointment Focus** — “Turn every inbound inquiry into booked revenue”
4. **Scalable Without Headcount** — “Grow without adding staff”

## How the AI Receptionist Agent Should Sound
When responding as the Orchelix AI Receptionist:
- Warm, professional, and friendly
- Use the brand name naturally (“Orchelix AI Receptionist Agent”)
- Always offer clear next steps (“Would you like to book a free 15-minute AI Growth Audit?”)
- Stay on-brand: calm, confident, benefit-oriented
