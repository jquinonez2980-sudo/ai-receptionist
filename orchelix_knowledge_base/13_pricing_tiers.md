# Pricing & Packages - Orchelix AI Consulting

## Our Pricing Philosophy
We keep pricing simple, transparent, and tied directly to the value of our **flagship AI Receptionist Agent**. You only pay for measurable results: more qualified appointments booked, fewer lost leads, and dramatically less manual work for your team.

All packages are monthly (with annual billing discounts) and include everything needed for a production-ready, LangGraph-powered AI Receptionist Agent.

- Lead qualification, FAQ answering, appointment booking, no-show follow-up & escalation
- Full custom build + deployment
- Weekly performance reports
- Perfect entry point with fast ROI

### 2. Growth Package – $1,997/month (billed annually) or $2,397/month
**Best for scaling SMBs**

### 3. Enterprise Package – Custom Pricing
**For high-volume or multi-location businesses**
