# Services & Pricing

## Botox — $12/unit (average $300-$600)
Billed per unit based on the treatment area and your provider's recommendation.
Requires a consultation with one of our providers before treatment — we never
book Botox directly.

## Filler — $650/syringe
Adds volume or smooths deeper lines. Requires a consultation with one of our
providers before treatment — we never book filler directly.

## HydraFacial — $175
A relaxing, non-invasive treatment that cleanses, exfoliates, and hydrates the
skin. No downtime.

## Laser Hair Removal — $99-$399/session
Price depends on the treatment area. A series of sessions is typically
recommended for best results — results vary by person.

## Body Contouring Consultation — Free
A no-cost, no-obligation consultation to discuss non-invasive body contouring
options with a provider.

## Payment
Cash, all major cards, and financing options available for larger treatment
packages.
